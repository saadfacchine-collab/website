US States Ripple Animation

Waves emanating from Kansas, spreading across the United States

Animation Details:

  • Origin: Kansas (center of the United States)
  • Effect: States light up in waves based on distance from Kansas
  • Duration: ~2 seconds total
  • Visual: Green fill with ripple effect and glow
  • Fallback: Uses simplified state shapes if GeoJSON fails to load

Technical Features:

  • D3.js: For data visualization and animations
  • GeoJSON: Real US state boundaries (with fallback)
  • Distance Calculation: Mathematical distance from Kansas
  • Ripple Effect: Expanding circles from each state center
  • Responsive: Adapts to container size
